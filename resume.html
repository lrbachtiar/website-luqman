Experience

As a high-school and university student, I worked part-time in sales as a Telemkarter and Sales Assistant. Communicating with clients and customers is a passion of mine and I thrive in high-pressure environments, consistently delivering quality service.

I have extensive experience in events coordination and networking for various student organisations, facilitating the communication between students and The University of Auckland’s Administrators.

My involvement with the University of Auckland also includes teaching roles for various engineering courses. These include courses such as mathematical modelling (ENGSCI 211), engineering computing (ENGSCI 131) and technical communications for engineers (ENGGEN 115).

Most of my technical work experience is with research institutes, in which I held research assistant roles. A number of research publications have been produced from these research projects.

I am currently employed with Orion Health Ltd as a Solution Engineer of Developer Operations. This role provides me with the first taste of working in the healthcare sector.
